find factorial of n no. number.

input | output
1     | 1 no factorial is 1;
2     | 2 no factorial is 2;
3     | 3 no factorial is 6;
4     | 4 no factorial is 24;
5     | 5 no factorial is 120;

........................................................

1 -> 1                 = 1
2 -> 1 * 2             = 2
3 -> 1 * 2 * 3         = 6
4 -> 1 * 2 * 3 * 4     = 24
5 -> 1 * 2 * 3 * 4 * 5 = 120
